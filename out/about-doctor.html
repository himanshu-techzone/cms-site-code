DR. MRINALINI SHARMA

MBBS, MS – General Surgery,
MCh – Plastic Surgery

Home/ About Doctor
  • State Topper – 1995
  • All India second rank in Mch Plastic Surgery Examination at PGIMER.
  • Best audio-visual presentation at Annual Conference of Association of Plastic Surgeons of India for “An objective analysis of smile in patients of facial palsy”

Dr. Mrinalini Sharma, is one of the top plastic surgeons practicing in Delhi-NCR. She is a qualified surgeon i.e. MBBS, MS and MCh with the medical experience of 11 years. She is a dedicated medical professional who has undergone extensive training in the management of cosmetic & plastic surgery treatments, also including maxillofacial surgeries, cancer reconstruction and micro-vascular surgery (including digital replantation) from PGIMER in Chandigarh. Dr. Mrinalini is also an expert in congenital anomalies like cleft palate, cleft lip, and hypospadias. Dr. Mrinalini has also worked with Indraprastha Apollo Hospitals, New Delhi and Columbia Asia Hospital, Gurgaon as a consultant plastic surgeon.

With passion for Plastic & Cosmetic Surgery and a motive to provide Plastic & Cosmetic Surgery treatments that everyone can afford, Dr. Mrinalini Sharma laid the foundation of Aestiva Plastic Surgery Clinic located in South Delhi. Being a female Plastic Surgeon, Dr. Mrinalini Sharma and the staff at Aestiva Clinic understands your concerns for safety and privacy. We provide high standards of hygiene and world class facilities at Aestiva Clinic.
